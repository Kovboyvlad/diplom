We need a software system for an ATM (Automated Teller Machine).

The customer inserts a card and enters a PIN code. If the PIN is entered incorrectly three times in a row, the card is blocked and retained. After successful login, the customer can withdraw cash, check their balance, deposit funds, or change their PIN.

For cash withdrawal, the system checks whether the account has sufficient funds and whether the ATM has enough cash available. After a successful transaction, a receipt is printed.

The ATM communicates with a bank server to verify the card and process transactions. Every operation is logged.

At the end of the session, the card is returned to the customer.
